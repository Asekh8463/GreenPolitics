GreenPolitics is a 39-question test that will try to tell you what kind of environmentalist you are based on 5 policies.

Read before starting:


This test will not work for you if you are a climate sceptic (read: straw-for-brains lunatic).

In this test I've used the terms "organizations" to refer to a non-governmental group of people fighintg climate change, and the term "fossil fuel" to refer to any non-renewable resource such as oil, gas, and coal.
